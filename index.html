3 Different Holidays

Chinese New Year

The Chinese New Year is also called the Lunar New Year. It is celebrated in the countries outside of China. It starts on the second new moon after the winter solstice and lasts for weeks! It is celebrated in a bunch a different forms such as fireworks and lion dancing!

Christmas

Christmas is a holiday that celebrates the birth of Jesus Christ. Christmas is celebrated in many different ways all over the world. In some countries they have a big feast with family and friends and in other countries they give gifts to eachother and etc.

Hanukkah

Every year, Jewish families gather together to celebrate Hanukkah. The holiday celebrates the miraculous life of a small quantity of oil that kept the menorah in the Second Temple in Jerusalem lit. In remembrance, Jews worldwide light menorahs for eight days every Hanukkah to celebrate. Families give gifts, play games of dreidel, and enjoy special foods all over the world.
